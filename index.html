Welcome to Saladin Wolves

We are a pack of veteran wolves with a great passion for Destiny 2 and its universe. The values that guide us are the following:

Communication, Respect, Common Sense, and Quality

Our Discord server is currently managing the following clans:

Saladin Wolves - Romanian
Iron Wolves I - International
Join us

BlackHamm3r is back and wants you to join Saladin Wolves. Join the man that started it all and his vision of developing the most powerful international community in Destiny 2.
We are the origin, and we will thrive together.

If you share our passion and wish to join our community, no matter your level of experience with the game, this is what we offer:

  • A happy group full of quality people, ready to help whenever you get stuck.
  • Wolves for weekly raids with a mix of experienced members (Sherpa) and newbies. Super chill atmosphere.
  • Wolves for PVP activities such as Trials, Survival and Iron Banner. We have members who are eager to team up with you.
  • Wolves for GrandMaster Nightfalls and other end-game activities with a high-level difficulty.

If you decided to join us, this is what we expect from you:

  • To be present in voice and text activities within our Discord server.
  • Attendance to events created with ClanEvents (Raids, GMs, Nightfalls, Gambit, PVP)
  • To support our community by voting and commenting on our weekly posts on Bungie & Reddit. (This is the best way for us to reach more players)

Rules and Regulations

Server Regulations

  1. Use the microphone mute option if you have background noise, to not disturb other members on the voice channels.
  2. Do your captcha verification in order to get one of the roles @WOLF , @Wolf Ally , @Guest.
  3. Register on @Charlemagne to have the roles assigned to your account.
  4. It is mandatory to keep the conversations in Romanian and also to use the channels properly when posting. Make sure you are chatting on the correct channel, replies should be relevant to each channel's topic.
  5. This server does not tolerate hate speech. You will be PERMANENTLY BANNED with no rights of appeal if we find evidence of excessive insults or toxic behavior.
  6. NO SELF-PROMOTION - This means there is no promotion of your own server, whether it's in DMs or directly on the server. This can result in a PERMANENT BAN from our server
  7. Swearing, bad jokes or racist statements are not tolerated on this server; as well as discussions about hacks, cheats etc. that can be perceived as encouraging or instructing others on how to use them.
  8. If you are found to be harassing or causing drama to any users on this server, you will be punished according to server regulations; we are not a TV server.
  9. Civil behavior should be encouraged at all times - No excessive NSFW or weird talk.
  10. Please report any member who abuses or violates the rules on support-ticket with sufficient evidence.
  • Your access to and use of the server is conditioned on your acceptance of and compliance with these rules. They apply to all users whether they are members of the clan @WOLF , @Wolf Ally or @Guest whom can access or use the Saladin Wolves Community.
  • We reserve the right to revoke this right (regardless of age or number of messages) if we deem it necessary.
  • At the time of registration, it is considered that you have read this regulation and you agree 100% with what is written here. If you are already registered when the rules are displayed, your continued posting constitutes tacit acceptance of the content or whether you have read it or not.
